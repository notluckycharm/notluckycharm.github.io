About Me

My name is Jacob Fernandes. I graduated from Harvard University (c/o '24) with a Joint Bachelors of Arts in Linguistics and Computer Science with Highest Honors, along with a language citation in Modern Japanese. My research interests include Japanese and Korean comparative lingustics, Theoretical Syntax (especially A-bar movement), and Natural Language Processing.

I am also interested in Linguistics fieldwork, and especially revitalization. I have fieldwork experience in languages from across several language families:

  • Alabama (Muskogean)
  • Georgian (Kartvelian)
  • Japanese
  • Korean
