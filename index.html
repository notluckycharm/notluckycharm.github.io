About Me

I'm Jacob Fernandes, a senior undergraduate student at Harvard University. I am a B.A candidate in Linguistics and Computer Science with a language citation in Japanese. My research interests include Japanese and Korean comparative lingustics, Theoretical Syntax (especially A-bar movement), and Natural Language Processing.

I'm also interested in Langauge Revitilization and research on Indigenous North American Languages. I am currently involved in the WOLF lab at Harvard University where I am a research assistant
